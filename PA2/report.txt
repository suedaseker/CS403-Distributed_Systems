Abstarct Classes:

Map and Reduce are the abstract methods for mapping and then reducing mapped data. 

Functions: 

Producer(producer input): Parts the input data and sends pieeces to the number of workers given.

Consumer(id): Receives data pieces, calls the mapping function, and then sends partial results to the Result Collector function.

ResultCollector(num workers): Aggregates partial results from the number of  workers and returns the final result.
